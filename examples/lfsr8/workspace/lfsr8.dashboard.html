lfsr8 — maximal-length Fibonacci LFSR (x⁸+x⁶+x⁵+x⁴+1, period 255)

—state (hex) · cycle 0

Each lamp is one bit of the state output of the synthesized netlist simulating in your browser (bit 7 left). The feedback bit is state[7]⊕state[5]⊕state[4]⊕state[3]; from a non-zero seed the register walks all 255 non-zero patterns. Nothing on this page is animated by its own logic — pause the clock and the lamps freeze.
