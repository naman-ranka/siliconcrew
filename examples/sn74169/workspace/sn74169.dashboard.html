A faithful ’169: all controls are active-low, exactly like the TTL part. LOAD̄=0 loads A on the clock edge; LOAD̄=1 with ENP̄=ENT̄=0 counts (U/D̄ high = up); anything else holds. The hatched lamps at power-on are honest — this netlist has no reset, so Q really is undefined until you load it. RCŌ pulses low at 15 counting up or 0 counting down.
