The real Simon FSM netlist, simulating in your browser: pads light from the design's led pins, the score digits are its multiplexed seven-segment bus (a digit dims when the design stops driving it), and the buzzer icon flickers when the sound pin is oscillating. The bundle is compiled with TICKS_PER_MILLI=1 so one clock cycle is one game-millisecond — 1.0× speed needs 1000 cycles/sec from your machine.
