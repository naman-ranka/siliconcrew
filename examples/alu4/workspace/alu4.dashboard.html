Toggle operand bits, pick an operation — the 8-bit result lamps are the netlist's registered result output (updates on the next clock edge, clock free-runs at 30 Hz). DIV packs {remainder, quotient}; ENC is (a≪4|b) ⊕ 0xAB. Carry/overflow are only meaningful for ADD/SUB.
