Pure combinational silicon — no clock. Every segment is the live Qa…Qg pin of the synthesized netlist; change any input and the decode propagates instantly. LT̄ (lamp test) and BĪ (blanking) are active-low as in a real 74xx-style decoder; RBĪ=0 blanks a zero (ripple blanking); AL sets the pins' active level — flip it and the raw pins invert, exactly as this page then shows.
