Found it. At t=240 the ALU inputs are a=AA, b=11 but y=BB. The opcode from decoder selects XOR instead of ADD — a one-bit mismatch in decoder.v's opcode map. The bug is cross-module (decoder → alu), which is why the unit test alu_tb passed but the integration test cpu_tb fails.
